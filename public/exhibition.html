Alfaaz has always been dedicated to promote and encourage art in the valley. We want to make it less biased and give chance and encourage new talent while sincerely appreciating our past participants.

New participants will get an advantage in the process of screening/selection, while others who have been part of our journey will be deemed to our new project "Tchanderwar".

How the Process Works

  • Step 1: Participants upload a PDF containing high-quality photographs of their recent works.
  • Step 2: Uploaded artworks are screened by the Alfaaz Curator.
  • Step 3: Selected participants are notified via email and dashboard with payment details to complete registration.

Compile up to 3 recent artworks into a single PDF before uploading.

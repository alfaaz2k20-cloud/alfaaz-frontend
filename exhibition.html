4th Annual Art Exhibition - 2024

Exhibition Date: 21st - 22nd Dec'24

Venue: Mahatta Art Gallery, Lal Chowk, Srinagar

Alfaaz has always been dedicated to promote and encourage art in the valley. We want to make it less biased and give chance and encourage new talent of the valley while sincerely appreciating our past participants.

New participants will get an advantage in the process of screening/selection, while others who have been part of our journey will be deemed to our new project "Tchanderwar". Further details about Tchanderwar will be disclosed at the upcoming event.

How the Process Works

  • Step 1: Participants will upload a PDF containing high-quality photographs of their recent works.
  • Step 2: Uploaded artworks will be screened by the Alfaaz Curator.
  • Step 3: Selected participants will be authorized via the Dashboard, where further details (participation fee, size, and number of artworks) will be shared.
